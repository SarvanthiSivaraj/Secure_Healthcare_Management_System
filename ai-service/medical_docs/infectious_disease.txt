Fever, chills, body aches, and fatigue are common symptoms of infectious diseases such as influenza or COVID-19.

Recommended department: General Medicine / Infectious Disease.

Symptoms:
- High fever
- Persistent cough
- Sore throat
- Muscle or body aches
- Loss of taste or smell

Patients with high, persistent fever (>103F or 39.4C) or difficulty breathing should seek immediate medical evaluation.
