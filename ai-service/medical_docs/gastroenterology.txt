Stomach pain, nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea are symptoms often related to gastrointestinal issues.

Recommended department: Gastroenterology.

Symptoms:
- Severe or persistent abdominal pain
- Nausea and vomiting
- Diarrhea or constipation
- Heartburn or acid reflux

Severe abdominal pain accompanied by fever or vomiting blood is a medical emergency.
