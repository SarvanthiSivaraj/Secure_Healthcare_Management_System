1. FEATURE OVERVIEW

---

Feature Name:
AI Medical Chatbot for Patient Pre-Screening

Purpose:
The AI chatbot assists patients before scheduling a medical visit by collecting symptoms through an interactive conversation, retrieving relevant medical knowledge from trusted documents, generating a structured medical summary, and recommending the appropriate medical department.

The chatbot DOES NOT diagnose diseases or prescribe medication.
It only performs pre-screening and triage assistance.

Benefits:

• Improves hospital triage efficiency
• Helps doctors review patient condition before consultation
• Prioritizes urgent cases
• Reduces workload on medical staff
• Improves patient interaction with the system

---

2. SYSTEM OBJECTIVES

---

1. Provide an interactive chatbot interface for patients.
2. Extract symptoms from natural language conversation.
3. Retrieve medical knowledge from trusted documents.
4. Ask dynamic follow-up questions.
5. Suggest the appropriate medical department.
6. Generate structured SOAP medical summaries.
7. Store triage sessions for doctor review.
8. Log chatbot decisions for audit and compliance.

13. SOAP MEDICAL SUMMARY GENERATION

---

After the conversation the chatbot generates a SOAP report.

SOAP format

S – Subjective
Patient symptoms and complaints.

O – Objective
Observed or measurable information.

A – Assessment
Possible condition or risk category.

P – Plan
Recommended department or next step.

Example

Subjective:
Patient reports chest pain and shortness of breath.

Objective:
Symptoms present for two hours.

Assessment:
Possible cardiac condition.

Plan:
Recommend cardiology consultation.
