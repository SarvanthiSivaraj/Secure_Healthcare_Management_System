Sudden numbness or weakness in the face, arm, or leg, especially on one side of the body, may indicate a stroke.

Recommended department: Neurology.

Symptoms:
- Severe headache with no known cause
- Confusion, trouble speaking, or difficulty understanding speech
- Trouble seeing in one or both eyes
- Trouble walking, dizziness, loss of balance, or lack of coordination

Stroke indicators are a medical emergency. Seek immediate medical attention.
